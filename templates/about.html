Redefining How Pre-Clearance Works

A digital platform designed to streamline and simplify customs pre-clearance processes for consignees. It enables users to efficiently manage shipment information, monitor processing status, and improve overall visibility throughout the clearance workflow. The system is built to enhance coordination between stakeholders by providing a centralized environment for document handling, submission tracking, and decision support.

By reducing manual processes and improving data accessibility, R3-PCR helps minimize delays and supports more accurate and timely processing. With a focus on reliability, transparency, and efficiency, R3-PCR empowers users to make informed decisions and ensures a smoother, more organized approach to shipment pre-clearance.

How It Works?

R3-PCR guides you through every step of the pre-clearance process from registration to BOC submission so you always know what's happening with your shipment.

1

Register

Create your account and fill in the registration form. Wait for supervisor approval via e-mail before you can log in.

2

Log In

Enter your username and password, then verify your identity using the One Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered e-mail.

3

Submit a Shipment

Complete all required fields and upload your supporting documents (Commercial Invoice, Packing List, AWB / BOL), then submit.

4

Leave Feedback

Once your shipment is Approved or Rejected, open the shipment detail, select a star rating (1–5), write a comment, and submit your feedback.

5

Monitor Status

Track your shipment's progress on the dashboard. You will receive a notification every time your shipment status changes.

Who is the Consignee?

The consignee is the importer or cargo owner expecting a shipment anywhere around the world. They submit documents to custom broker for pre-clearance processing.
