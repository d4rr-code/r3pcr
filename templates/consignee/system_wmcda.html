How MCDA Works

  • Cost — Weighs total landed cost (freight + duties + fees); higher weight favors the most cost-efficient shipping mode
  • Time — Weighs urgency level (Rush/Urgent/Normal); higher weight favors faster modes like Air
  • Weight — Weighs gross cargo weight; higher weight prioritizes modes suited for heavier shipments such as FCL
  • Distance — Weighs transport route distance; higher weight prioritizes shorter transit routes to destination
Note: The MCDA recommendation appears on your computation advisory page for each shipment. Weights may be manually configured or derived from expert pairwise comparisons using Saaty AHP. Contact the supervisor to adjust these weights.
