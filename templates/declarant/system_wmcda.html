How WMCDA Works

  • Distance Weight — Higher weight prioritizes shorter transit routes (Air/Land over Sea)
  • Urgency Weight — Higher weight emphasizes speed for time-critical shipments (Rush/Urgent)
  • Cargo Weight — Higher weight factors gross weight into mode cost-efficiency calculation
  • Cargo Volume — Higher weight factors CBM into container utilization (LCL vs FCL decision)
ℹ️ Note: The WMCDA recommendation appears on your computation advisory page for each shipment. Contact the supervisor to adjust these weights.
